Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Look at all that cheese! Some people say you don't need that much, but I disagree.

This macaroni and cheese recipe will bring you to your knees and make you say "Hallejujah!". At least that's what I do.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz. Macaroni Noodles
  • 2 cans of Evaporated Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • 2 sticks of Salted Butter
  • Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • Literally any other cheeses you like

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water
  2. Throw your noodles in the pot and cook until done
  3. Drain water from your noodles
  4. Add two sticks of butter to noodles and stir until melted
  5. Add mild cheddar cheese and mozzarella, stir until mixed
  6. Add one can of evaporated milk, stir until mixed
  7. Add remaining cheese and can of evaporated milk, stir until mixed
  8. Crack and beat eggs, then add to noodles and stir until mixed
  9. Spray cooking pan with PAM
  10. Add macaroni mixture to pan
  11. Bake at 375 degrees for approximately 45 minutes to an hour
  12. Eat until you pass out
