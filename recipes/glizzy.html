Classic Glizzy

Description

We all love franks. We all love dawgs. But me personally? I'm all about the glizzies! Yerr!

Ingredients

  • 1 Pack of Nathan's Franks
  • Brioche Hot Dog Buns
  • Yellow Mustard

Steps

  1. Slice that pack of glizzies open and put them on a plate
  2. Take them outside, bust out your grill and warm that thing up to abuot 375 degrees
  3. Throw those glizzies on the grill, let them get those nice grill marks on one side
  4. Flip your glizzies and get the other side with those grill marks
  5. Let them relax until they're charred enough for you, then take them off the grill
  6. Bring the glizzies inside, throw them on top of those tasty brioche buns
  7. Hit them with some yellow mustard and get to gobblin'!
